Order Cancellation Policy


We understand that sometimes plans change. If you wish to cancel an order, we're here to assist you. Please take note of our cancellation policy below:

Cancellation Timeframe:

You may cancel your order, free of charge, within 12 hours of placing it.

Cancellation Process:

To cancel your order, please call our dedicated cancellation hotline at 0187-585-7959 during 11pm to 1 am. Our customer support team will be happy to assist you with the cancellation process.

Important Considerations:

  1. Cancellation requests made after the 12-hour window will not be accepted.
  2. Make sure to have your order number and relevant details ready when you call for cancellation to expedite the process.
  3. Once your order has been successfully canceled within the stipulated time, we will process a full refund to the original payment method used during the purchase.


At Blocke.Shop, we value your satisfaction and strive to provide the best customer service possible. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team.
